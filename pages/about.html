I'm currently enrolled in a web design class at Brigham Young University!

But my childhood memories of playing "Age of Mythology" heavily influenced the logistics game as well. As an homage to those days, 50% of the sound effects are directly from "Age of Mythology".

Sovereign is all about Balance.

There are a few ways to lose, but a whole bunch of factors that can lead to them.

Ways you can LOSE:

            Starving to death!

            Invading army kills everyone!

            Your citizen's kick you out because satisfaction is less then 20%!

Best to avoid any of those happening.

Click the Buttons Below to Find out How to Not Lose!

Full Farms means Full Bellies

Food is pretty easy to come by.

Your ownly outsource is the citizen needs, which is one per month per citizen.

Hunting for your food seems pretty ideal.

However famines are a possiblity.

Famines:

            Farms make 30% less.

            Citizens need x2 as much food.

            Famines last 3 months.

Because of this uncontrolable event, it's best to keep farms well staffed.

            Additionally Robbers have a chance of taking food.

            And lost invasions will take food as well.

BE PREPARED

Invasions start happening after you go past 30 citizens.

The enemy strength is determined by your population.

The more people you have, the more you should invest in defences.

Ways to Defend

            Weapons allow regular citizens to fight.

            Soldiers cost a monthly wage.

            Archers cost a monthly wage.

            Catapults require two soldiers to man and wages.

            Build up walls for big defensive boosts.

            Hire Mercinaries to be safe for three months.

Should you lose an invasion, you're kingdom is ransacked of its resources.

Deaths are calculated by how bad you lost.

Better defenses mean less deaths.

After every invasion, soldiers, archers and catapults are the first to die.

So remember to restrengthen after every battle!

Happy town!

Satisfaction is based on a bunch of differenct factors.

Some of them are out of your control, so keep them happy!

Ways to HAPPY

            Hold feasts or town faires for temporary boosts.

            The Market, Town Square and Tavern give perminant boosts.

            Keep those taxes low!

            Be victorious in battle for boosts! Lose and citizens will be sad.

            Certain Special Items help with satisfaction.

Disasters, bad special items, high taxes, losing to invasions are all things that could get you booted from town.

Cheat code is available to disable death by satisfaction.
